Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Decision Maker is designed as a simple browser-based app. This version does not require an account and does not send your decisions to a server.

2. Information You Enter

Questions, options, results, favorites, and history stay in your browser when you save them. They are stored with browser local storage on your device.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

This static version does not collect names, email addresses, payment details, precise location, contacts, or account credentials.

4. Local Storage

The app uses local storage to remember decision history, favorites, and your selected theme. You can remove this data by using the Clear button in the app where available or by clearing your browser data.

5. Sharing

If you use the Share button, your browser or device may open its native sharing tools. Any information you share is handled by the app or service you choose to share with.

6. Cookies and Analytics

This version does not use cookies, advertising trackers, or analytics scripts. If those services are added later, this policy should be updated before launch.

7. Children

Decision Maker is intended for general audiences making everyday choices. Children should use it with guidance from a parent or guardian.

8. Security

Because saved decisions are stored locally in your browser, anyone with access to your device or browser profile may be able to see them.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated as the website changes. The updated date at the top will show when the policy was last revised.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the website owner or developer responsible for publishing this site.
